Welcome to an example Social Progress Component Map for London based on Publicly available data.

The two components available are

  1. Health and Wellness and
  2. Personal Safety

These components have been modelled on the Barking and Dagenham 2018 SPI and contain the indicators that are available at ward level across London Boroughs.
